CANAL TOWNS

Game Studio
Coconut Island was founded on April 15, 2010 and belongs to Shanghai Kena Information Technology Co., Ltd. Coconut Island is a game research and development publisher. It focuses on game production promotes the development of independent games in China. IOS, Android, Xbox one, PS4, and Steam platforms have been covered.
Real World
Canal towns are generally concentrated in the Jiangnan area which includes the Jiangsu province, Zhejiang province and Shanghai area. They are located towards the south of the Yangtze River. These river-based settlements are widely advertised as tourism destinations because of their historical and cultural representations of China.
